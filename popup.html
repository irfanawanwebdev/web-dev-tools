See the current page the way people with color vision deficiencies do. The filter is applied live to the page and to the swatches below. Around 1 in 12 men and 1 in 200 women have some form of color blindness.

Responsive Viewport Tester

Preview the current page at any device viewport, including phone widths a real window can't shrink to. It opens a scaled, exact-size preview in a new tab where you can switch sizes, rotate, zoom, toggle a mobile user-agent, and take a full-page screenshot at that viewport. Works on any site.
